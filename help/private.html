Use the 'Private' checkbox to send chat notes to yourself.
You may use this function for notes on moves, strategies, your opponent, etc.
These notes will be shown in red and only be shown to you, not your opponent.
If you intend to send a note to yourself, it is recommended that you select the checkbox before entering your message to avoid sending a 'chat' viewable by your opponent.
There is also a small unavoidable bug where, if you do not check the private checkbox first, your message may be lost due to a page refresh when you do select the checkbox.
